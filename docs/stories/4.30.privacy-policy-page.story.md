# Story 4.30: Privacy Policy Page

## Status
Draft

## Story
**As a** user,  
**I want** to access the privacy policy,  
**so that** I understand how my personal data is collected, used, and protected.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Privacy Policy page accessible via /#/legal/privacy route
2. Page displays full privacy policy text
3. Content includes standard sections:
   - Data controller information
   - Types of data collected
   - Purpose of data processing
   - Legal basis for processing
   - Data retention
   - User rights (GDPR rights)
   - Data security
   - Third-party services
   - Cookies and tracking
   - Contact information for privacy inquiries
4. Page uses design system styling (Minimalist Monochrome)
5. Link to privacy policy available in footer and cookie banner
6. Last updated date displayed
7. Print-friendly formatting
8. Scrollable content area
