### Research: Additional Official Sources

#### 1. BAMF (Bundesamt für Migration und Flüchtlinge)
- **Agency:** Federal Office for Migration and Refugees
- **URL:** https://www.bamf.de/EN/Themen/MigrationAufenthalt/ZuwandererDrittstaaten/Migrathek/migrathek-node.html
- **Alternative URL:** https://www.bamf.de/EN/Themen/MigrationAufenthalt/ZuwandererDrittstaaten/Migrathek/migrathek-node.html
- **Visa Type:** Work (immigration procedures, residence permits)
- **Fetch Type:** HTML
- **What it Monitors:** Immigration procedures, residence permit requirements, integration information
- **Uniqueness:** Provides detailed procedural information and integration requirements not covered by BMI/Auswärtiges Amt
- **Feasibility:** Publicly accessible, HTML content, standard government website structure
- **Status:** Official government agency website
- **Notes:** BAMF handles immigration procedures and provides detailed information on residence permits

#### 2. DAAD (Deutscher Akademischer Austauschdienst)
- **Agency:** German Academic Exchange Service
- **URL:** https://www.daad.de/en/study-and-research-in-germany/plan-your-studies/entry-and-residence/
- **Visa Type:** Student
- **Fetch Type:** HTML
- **What it Monitors:** Student visa requirements, entry requirements, residence permits for students
- **Uniqueness:** Official academic exchange service with detailed student-specific immigration information
- **Feasibility:** Publicly accessible, HTML content, well-structured website
- **Status:** Official government-affiliated organization (funded by German government)
- **Notes:** DAAD is the official German organization for international academic cooperation, provides comprehensive student visa information

#### 3. Bundesagentur für Arbeit (BA) - ZAV
- **Agency:** Federal Employment Agency - International Job Placement
- **URL:** https://www.arbeitsagentur.de/en/working-in-germany
- **Alternative URL:** https://www.zav.de/en
- **Visa Type:** Work
- **Fetch Type:** HTML
- **What it Monitors:** Work permit requirements, job placement services, employment-related immigration
- **Uniqueness:** Provides employment agency perspective on work permits and job placement
- **Feasibility:** Publicly accessible, HTML content
- **Status:** Official government agency
- **Notes:** Bundesagentur für Arbeit handles work permits and provides employment-related immigration information
