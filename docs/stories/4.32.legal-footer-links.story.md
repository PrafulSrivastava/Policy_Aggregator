# Story 4.32: Legal Footer Links Integration

## Status
Draft

## Story
**As a** user,  
**I want** easy access to all legal pages from the footer,  
**so that** I can find legal information and comply with requirements.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Footer displays organized legal links section
2. Links include:
   - Terms & Conditions
   - Privacy Policy
   - Cookie Policy
   - Impressum / Legal Information
   - FAQ (optional, can be in separate section)
3. All links use hash-based routing (/#/legal/*)
4. Links open in same window (no external links)
5. Footer section styled according to design system
6. Links are accessible (keyboard navigation, screen readers)
7. Footer persists across all pages
8. Mobile-responsive layout
