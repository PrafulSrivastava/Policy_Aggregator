# Story 4.27: Terms and Conditions Page

## Status
Draft

## Story
**As a** user,  
**I want** to access terms and conditions,  
**so that** I understand the legal terms of using the service.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Terms and Conditions page accessible via /#/legal/terms route
2. Page displays full terms and conditions text
3. Content includes standard sections:
   - Service description
   - User obligations
   - Limitation of liability
   - Intellectual property
   - Termination
   - Governing law
4. Page uses design system styling (Minimalist Monochrome)
5. Link to terms available in footer
6. Last updated date displayed
7. Print-friendly formatting
8. Scrollable content area
